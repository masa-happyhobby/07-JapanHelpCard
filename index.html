App Store Review Page

Show Japanese help cards when you need assistance in Japan.

Japan Help Card helps foreign travelers, students, workers, and residents communicate common problems in Japanese without typing or speaking fluent Japanese.

App Name

Japan Help Card

Business Model

Free basic cards + one-time Pro purchase

Purpose

Designed for non-food trouble in Japan

The app is a collection of simple cards users can show to people nearby. Each card displays a clear Japanese phrase plus supporting English context.

Medical and daily help

Cards for fever, stomach pain, ambulance requests, hospital search, restroom, Wi-Fi, charging, and general help.

Travel and public safety

Cards for trains, taxis, lost wallet, lost passport, police station, hotel communication, disasters, and evacuation shelters.

Clear app boundary

Food allergy and dietary restriction cards are intentionally handled by the separate app Allergy Card Japan.

Allergy Card Japan

Food allergy cards are not duplicated here

Japan Help Card is intentionally focused on medical, transport, lost item, disaster, hotel, shopping, and daily trouble cards. If a user searches for allergy or dietary restriction keywords, the app recommends Allergy Card Japan instead.

In-App Purchase

Lifetime Pro is a one-time purchase

Pro is implemented as a non-consumable in-app purchase. There is no subscription plan in the app.

Free version

  • Basic help cards
  • Quick SOS cards
  • Emergency numbers in Japan
  • Allergy Card Japan recommendation

Lifetime Pro

  • Multiple language translations
  • Custom cards
  • Emergency profile notes
  • Voice playback
  • Offline-ready saved card use cases
  • PDF/export and sharing-related features

Privacy and Safety

Optional personal information stays local

The app does not require account registration. Optional personal information, such as hotel name, emergency contact, medical notes, medicines, and custom cards, is stored locally on the user's device.

No account required

Users can open and use the core card experience without creating an account.

Local-first notes

Custom cards and emergency profile notes are optional and intended for device-local storage.

Not professional advice

The app helps communication only. It does not provide medical diagnosis, legal advice, or emergency service advice.
